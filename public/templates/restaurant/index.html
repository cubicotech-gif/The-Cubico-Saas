Our Story

Nestled in the heart of Karachi, Flavor House was born from a simple belief: that extraordinary dining is where culinary artistry meets heartfelt hospitality. Since 2018, we have been crafting dishes that honor tradition while embracing the bold flavors of modern gastronomy.

Our kitchen is led by Chef Ayesha Malik, whose journeys through Istanbul, Marrakech, and Lyon have shaped a menu that tells a story with every plate. We source locally wherever we can — from the freshest catch at the harbor to organic herbs grown on our rooftop garden.

The ambiance at Flavor House is designed to feel like home, yet unlike any home you have visited. Warm terracotta walls, soft candlelight, and curated jazz create an atmosphere where time slows down and conversations come alive.

— Chef Ayesha Malik, Founder
